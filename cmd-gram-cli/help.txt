Welcome to cmd-gram-cli! Available commands:

/login           - Log in to your account by entering email and password.
/reg             - Register a new account with your email and password.
/new-chat        - Create a new chat by providing a user email.
/all-chats       - List all your available chats.
/open-chat [n]   - Open a chat by its index in the chat list (replace [n] with the chat number).

Use /exit in any chat to leave it.
If you're unsure about what to do, use the /help command.

Note: Make sure to log in in before creating or opening chats.
